What is Cyber Security?

Cybersecurity is the ongoing effort to protect the networked systems and all of the data from unauthorized use or harm.


Cyber security is the practice of defcending computer, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks and data from malicious attacks. It is also known as information technology security. It is also the convergence of people, processes and technology that come together to protect organizations, individuals or networks from digital attacks.

Basic Objective of this Course

This expert course is designed to give in detail knowledge about Cyber Security.


After completing this course, you will be able to-

  • Design, Develop, test and evaluate secure software.
  • Develop polices and procedures to manage enterprise security risks.
