What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking is legally breaking into computers and devices to test an organization's defenses.


Ethical hacking involves an authorized attempt to gain unauthorized acces to a computer system, application or data. Carrying out ethical hacking involves duplicating strategies and actions of malicious attackers. People who do it are called ethical hackers, security experts that perform these assessments.
